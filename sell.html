Become a seller

Upload an item

Easy to get started

Next you will only need to upload your item along with a screenshot, fill out some basic information, and set your price. Once it's been reviewed and approved, your item will appear for sale.

Earn 80% of the Item Price

Sellers receive 80% of the Item Price for items sold exclusively and 40% for items sold non-exclusively. See detailed information about the fee structure on WrapBootstrap.

Secured downloads

When someone buys your item they're sent a specially-crafted download link that expires within 24 hours. This helps if the download link ends up in a forum. After the time limit, the link expires.

Build your brand

As a seller you'll have the opportunity to build-up your personal brand and set yourself apart by uploading a header image and filling out the about section on your seller profile.

Meet new customers

Your customers are your own; we're not locking you in. Purchasing from WrapBootstrap opens a dialog with the seller and you are encouraged to convert your customer into a loyal fan.

Unsaturated market

The number of people who want Bootstrap templates is much greater than the number that exist. Seize this opportunity to be one of the best designers to sell to this market!
